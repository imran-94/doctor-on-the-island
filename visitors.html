Hotel Visits for Visitors in Zanzibar

We bring English-speaking doctors to your hotel in Zanzibar — fast, private, and professional. Book a hotel visit by WhatsApp or phone and a clinician will arrive to examine, treat and provide prescriptions or referrals.

visitors & hotel visits

Who this is for

Tourists, expats and short-term visitors in Zanzibar who need medical attention at their hotel — from food poisoning and dehydration to minor injuries and medication refills.

Common issues we treat

  • Food poisoning and stomach upsets
  • Dehydration and heat exhaustion
  • Minor wounds, cuts and sutures
  • Fever, coughs and respiratory symptoms
  • Prescription refills and travel medicine advice

How a hotel visit works

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp or call — provide hotel name, room number, and short symptoms.
  2. Confirm — we send an estimated arrival window and consultation fee.
  3. Visit — an English-speaking doctor visits the hotel, examines and treats.
  4. Payment — by M-Pesa, cash or card (card on request). We issue a receipt and prescription if needed.

Fees (sample)

Consultation at hotel: USD 25–35 (or TSh 60,000–85,000) inside Makunduchi/nearby. Prices confirmed on contact. Travel surcharge applies for remote locations.
